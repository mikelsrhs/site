Do you believe honesty, compassion, respect, responsibility, and courage are necessary components of good citizenship?

All of these qualities are great characteristics to have although they don't all fall under the category of a good citizen

Honesty

Honesty relates to being genuine and authentic, often telling the truth, and being consistent. Honesty is linked to trust amongst your peers and allows for genuine conversation.

Compassion

Compassion is a great quality to have but it not necessary to citizenship.

Respect

It is important to have and show respect for others along with yourself. Linking back to honesty, if a person is genuine and tells the truth, they are showing their uttermost respect which is vital to citizenship

Responsibility

Responsibility is crucial to good citizenship. People must take responsibilty for their actions which also involves being honest.

Courage

Courage relates to good morals which is a great attribute. Coruage often means doing acts that may be unpopular but are meant to help others

Contribution

Contributing or giving back to society is an important and often forgotten componnent of being a good citizen. Whether it ivolves protecting the environment or donating money to charity, contribution is fundamental to citizenship

Act of good citizenship

Going to a third world country such as Africa or Latin America and helping those in poverty build homes or schools, giving them shoes to protect their feet, or provide healthy food options.
