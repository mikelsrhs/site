PNG: stands for Portable Network Graphic. A png was created as a replacement for gif files. With the png file, the quality of the image will not change but more often than not, the image will be larger. They are best used for line drawings and text. JPG: stands for Joint Photographic Experts Group. jpg is the most common format for storing images from the internet. jpg is also the most common image format for images taken by a digital camera such as an iPhone. GIF: stands for Graphics Interchange Format. They are best for animated pictures with no audio. A gif often reduces the size of the file while keeping the quality the same.
